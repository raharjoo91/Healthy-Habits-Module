Understanding Overweight & Obesity

Building awareness and motivation for positive change

📚 What is Overweight & Obesity?

Overweight and obesity are conditions where excess body fat has accumulated to the extent that it may have a negative effect on health and well-being.

For adolescents, these conditions are determined by comparing your body mass index (BMI) to age and gender-specific growth charts. Being overweight or obese increases the risk of developing various health problems both now and in the future.

🔍 Causes and Contributing Factors

🍔

Unhealthy Eating Patterns

Poor dietary choices and irregular eating habits contribute significantly to weight gain.

Common Examples:

  • Frequent consumption of fast food
  • High intake of sugary drinks
  • Large portion sizes
  • Skipping meals leading to overeating
📱

Sedentary Lifestyle

Lack of physical activity and excessive screen time reduce calorie burning.

Common Examples:

  • Spending hours on devices
  • Limited physical education
  • Preference for indoor activities
  • Lack of active transportation
🧬

Environmental & Genetic Factors

Family history, environment, and social influences play important roles.

Common Examples:

  • Family eating patterns
  • Genetic predisposition
  • Social and cultural factors
  • Economic constraints

⚠️ Health Consequences

Cardiovascular Health

Increased risk of heart disease, high blood pressure, and stroke in adulthood.

🍯

Diabetes Risk

Higher likelihood of developing type 2 diabetes and insulin resistance.

😟

Psychological Impact

Potential effects on self-esteem, body image, and mental health.
