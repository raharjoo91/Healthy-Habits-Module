Healthy Habits Module

School-Based Intervention for Adolescent Health

Welcome to Your Health Journey

This comprehensive module is designed to empower adolescents with evidence-based strategies to reduce overweight and obesity through interactive learning, behavioral change techniques, and peer support systems. Based on Social Cognitive Theory, this intervention provides practical tools for building healthy habits that last a lifetime.
