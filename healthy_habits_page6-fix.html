🧠 Understanding Mental Health

The Mind-Body Connection

Mental health is just as important as physical health. Your thoughts, emotions, and stress levels directly impact your eating habits, energy levels, and motivation for healthy behaviors.

🍫

Stress & Eating

Stress can trigger emotional eating, cravings for unhealthy foods, and disrupted meal patterns

⚡

Mood & Motivation

Low mood and anxiety can reduce motivation for exercise and healthy lifestyle choices

😴

Sleep & Health

Poor mental health affects sleep quality, which impacts hunger hormones and weight management

💪

Self-Esteem & Habits

Positive self-image and confidence support long-term healthy behavior maintenance

💡 Did you know? Exercise is as effective as medication for mild depression!
